Grilled Soy-Glazed Salmon with Lemon, Pepper, and Vegetable Salsa



Recipe Title: Grilled Soy-Glazed Salmon with Lemon, Pepper, and Vegetable Salsa

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets
- 1 lemon
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

Instructions:

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Cut the lemon in half and squeeze the juice from one half into a small bowl.
3. Add the black pepper and soy sauce to the bowl and mix well.
4. Brush the soy glaze onto both sides of the salmon fillets and set aside.
5. In a separate bowl, combine the diced bell peppers and cherry tomatoes to make the salsa.
6. Cut the remaining half of the lemon into small wedges and add them to the salsa.
7. Season the salsa with a pinch of black pepper and mix well.
8. Place the salmon fillets on the preheated grill, skin side down.
9. Grill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
10. While the salmon is cooking, place the salsa in a small saucepan and heat over medium-low heat.
11. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are slightly softened.
12. Remove the salmon from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
13. Serve the salmon topped with the warm vegetable salsa and a squeeze of lemon juice.
14. Enjoy your delicious and healthy Grilled Soy-Glazed Salmon with Lemon, Pepper, and Vegetable Salsa!
